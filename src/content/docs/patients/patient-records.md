Efficiently managing patient information is core to your clinic. This guide covers how to create, update, and organize patient records.

## Creating a new patient record

1. Navigate to **Patients** from the sidebar
2. Click **Add Patient**
3. Enter patient details:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Contact information
   - Medicare/insurance details
4. Click **Save**

## Updating patient information

1. Search for the patient using the search bar
2. Click on their name to open their profile
3. Click **Edit** to modify details
4. Save your changes

## Patient history

View a patient's complete history including:

- Previous appointments
- Clinical notes
- Prescriptions
- Referrals
- Billing history
