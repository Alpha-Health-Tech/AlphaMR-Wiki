Learn how to create, modify, and manage appointments in your clinic.

## Creating a new appointment

1. Navigate to the **Bookings** section
2. Click **New Appointment**
3. Select the patient (or create a new patient record)
4. Choose the date and time
5. Select the practitioner
6. Add any notes if needed
7. Click **Save**

## Modifying appointments

To reschedule or modify an existing appointment:

1. Find the appointment in your calendar
2. Click on the appointment to open details
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Update**

## Cancelling appointments

To cancel an appointment:

1. Open the appointment details
2. Click **Cancel Appointment**
3. Optionally add a cancellation reason
4. Confirm the cancellation
