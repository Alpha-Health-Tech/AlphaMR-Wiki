Customize AlphaMR to match your clinic's needs.

## General settings

Access settings from the gear icon in the sidebar:

- **Clinic profile** - Name, address, logo
- **Working hours** - Set operating days and times
- **Appointment types** - Define consultation types and durations
- **Notification preferences** - Email and SMS settings

## User management

Manage your team:

1. Go to **Settings > Users**
2. Click **Invite User**
3. Enter their email and select a role
4. Send the invitation

### User roles

- **Admin** - Full access to all features
- **Practitioner** - Clinical features, own patients
- **Receptionist** - Bookings and patient management
- **Billing** - Invoicing and payments only
