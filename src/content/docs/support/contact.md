Our support team is here to help you with any questions or issues.

## Contact options

### Email Support
Send us an email at **support@alphamr.com** and we'll respond within 24 hours.

### Live Chat
Available Monday to Friday, 9am - 5pm AEST. Click the chat icon in the bottom right corner of your dashboard.

### Phone Support
Call us at **1800 XXX XXX** during business hours for urgent issues.

## Before contacting support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please have ready:

1. Your clinic name and account email
2. A description of the issue
3. Screenshots if applicable
4. Steps you've already tried

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I reset my password?**
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**How do I add a new staff member?**
Go to Settings > Users > Invite User. See our [user management guide](/alphamr-wiki/settings/clinic-settings/).

**How do I export patient data?**
Go to Patients > Export and select your export options.
