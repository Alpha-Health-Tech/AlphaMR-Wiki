---
title: Managing GPs & Referrals
description: Manage GP relationships and patient referrals
---

Keep track of referring GPs and manage the referral process efficiently.

## Adding a GP

1. Go to **GPs & Referrals** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add GP**
3. Enter GP details:
   - Name and provider number
   - Practice name
   - Contact details
4. Click **Save**

## Linking a GP to a patient

1. Open the patient's record
2. Go to the **GP** tab
3. Search for and select the GP
4. Save the association

## Creating a referral

1. From a patient's record, click **New Referral**
2. Select the referring GP
3. Add referral details and notes
4. Generate and send the referral letter
