---
title: Invoicing & Payments
description: Create invoices and manage payments in Alpha Medical Records
---

Manage your clinic's billing, invoicing, and payment tracking.

## Creating an invoice

1. Navigate to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Invoice**
3. Select the patient
4. Add line items:
   - Consultation fees
   - Procedures
   - Other charges
5. Review the total
6. Click **Generate Invoice**

## Sending invoices

Once generated, you can:

- **Email** the invoice directly to the patient
- **Print** a physical copy
- **Download** as PDF

## Recording payments

1. Open the invoice
2. Click **Record Payment**
3. Enter payment details:
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Date
4. Save

## Insurance claims

For patients with insurance:

1. Create the invoice as normal
2. Select **Submit Claim**
3. Review claim details
4. Submit electronically
